Welcome to AI mode, feel free to adjust the paddle's AI fields.


The paddle's view distance is how far it can see in either direction.
0 means it can't see, 100 means it can see the entire game.
Visualized by two horizontal green lines.


The paddle's body awareness is how well it realizes the size of bodies.
0 means that the paddle thinks bodies are points on the xy plane;
100 means that the paddle is perfectly aware body sizes;
in between values as they increase start to predict body size with more accuracy.
Visualized by yellow rectangles around bodies.


The paddle's trajectory prediction is how it predicts where to go based on ball trajectory.
0 means that it only knows the current gamestate with no reference of time;
100 means that the paddle is tracking the balls trajectory over time perfectly;
in between values as they increase get better at predicting trajectory.
Visualized by ball trails.
